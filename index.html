About

Sameera Tillman is an accomplished professional serving as Secretary on the Board of Directors of the Fayette County Development Authority (FCDA). Her journey from New York State Soccer Champion to NYU graduate to community leader exemplifies her commitment to excellence in everything she pursues.

A graduate of New York University, Sameera has demonstrated leadership from an early age — from leading student organizations to achieving athletic excellence at the state level. Today, she applies those same skills to support responsible economic growth and community development in Fayette County, Georgia.

Role & Community Impact

As Board Secretary, Sameera Tillman supports the governance functions of the Fayette County Development Authority, ensuring transparency and consistency in the organization's mission to foster responsible economic growth.

The Fayette County Development Authority supports business retention, expansion initiatives, and long-range planning — working with state and local partners to create opportunities for sustainable community growth. FCDA's work includes supporting developments like Trilith, which brings film production and related industries to the region.
